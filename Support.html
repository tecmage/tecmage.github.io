Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

On first launch, the app will download a ~350 MB AI voice model from HuggingFace. This is a one-time download. Once complete, you can start reading by entering a URL, opening a file, or pasting text.

How do I read a web article?

Enter a URL in the input field at the top of the app and tap the play button. The app will extract the article content and begin reading.

What file types are supported?

KO Reader supports:

  • Web URLs (articles from any website)
  • EPUB files
  • PDF files
  • Plain text

How do I adjust the reading speed?

Open Settings and use the speed slider to adjust playback from 0.5x to 2.0x.

How do I use the Share extension?

In Safari, tap the Share button and select "KO Reader" from the share sheet. The app will open and begin reading the page.

Why does the app need to download a model?

KO Reader uses on-device AI for text-to-speech generation. The model is downloaded once from HuggingFace and stored locally on your device. No internet connection is required after the initial download.

How do I change the voice?

Open Settings and select "Voice" to browse and preview available voices.

How does the sleep timer work?

Tap the moon icon in the player controls to set a sleep timer. When the timer expires, playback will stop automatically.

Is my data being collected?

No. KO Reader processes everything on your device. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.
